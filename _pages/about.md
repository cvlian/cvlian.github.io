======
I'm a M. S. candidate in School of EECS at Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST), advised by Prof. Hyuk Lim. I received the B.S. degree from the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, GIST, in 2019, where I'm currently pursuing the M.S. degree. My research interests include network-based intrusion detection, cryptography, and integration of cyber defense frameworks with deep learning architectures.
